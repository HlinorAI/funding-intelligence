# Mechanism types

The agent must not call every route a grant.

| Type | Meaning | What it pays for | Typical proof |
|---|---|---|---|
| `proposal_grant` | Pre-application review with milestones and reporting | Public goods, infrastructure, targeted ecosystem work | Scope, repository, milestones, budget |
| `retro` | Ship first, then seek a reward | Demonstrated impact | Live deployment, usage, adoption |
| `incentive` | Rewards for activity, liquidity, or network outcomes | TVL, volume, users, transactions | Economics, tracking, retained usage |
| `accelerator` | Selection of a team and product | Capital, mentorship, distribution, investor access | Product, market, team, traction |
| `investment` | Equity, SAFE, SAFT, or convertible investment | Venture-scale company | Company, market, traction, terms readiness |
| `subsidy` | Reimbursement of a specific cost | Audit, gas, security, or integration cost | Mature scope, provider, deployment commitment |
| `bd` | Relationship-driven partnership | Pilot, integration, customers, distribution | Named workload, champion, KPI |

Do not combine these into one total:

- non-dilutive grant;
- investment;
- liquidity or incentive rewards;
- audit or security subsidy;
- strategic BD value.
