# Mechanism types

Агент не называет все маршруты грантами.

| Type | Meaning | What it pays for | Typical proof |
|---|---|---|---|
| `proposal_grant` | Предварительная заявка с review, milestones и reporting | Public goods, infra, targeted ecosystem work | Scope, repo, milestones, budget |
| `retro` | Сначала shipped work, потом reward | Уже доказанный impact | Live deployment, usage, adoption |
| `incentive` | Rewards за activity, liquidity или network outcomes | TVL, volume, users, transactions | Economics, tracking, retained usage |
| `accelerator` | Отбор команды и продукта | Capital, mentorship, distribution, investor access | Product, market, team, traction |
| `investment` | Инвестиция с equity/SAFE/SAFT/convertible terms | Venture-scale company | Company, market, traction, terms readiness |
| `subsidy` | Возмещение конкретной стоимости | Audit, gas, security или integration cost | Mature scope, provider, deployment commitment |
| `bd` | Relationship-driven partnership | Pilot, integration, customers, distribution | Named workload, champion, KPI |

Не складывать в один total:

- non-dilutive grant;
- investment;
- liquidity/incentive rewards;
- audit/security subsidy;
- strategic BD value.
