# Stop conditions

Stop writing the application and route elsewhere when:

- official source confirms the program is closed or the intake endpoint is absent;
- a `VERIFY` route cannot be confirmed after a focused status check;
- the project has no native technical or distribution advantage and would need an expensive rewrite;
- required proof is not present and can be produced only by shipping first;
- no champion appears after three high-quality BD follow-ups;
- the expected net value of incentives is negative after liquidity, acquisition, volatility, security and opportunity costs;
- the ask is materially larger than the mechanism's normal milestone scope;
- the proposed outcome cannot be measured within the program's review window;
- institutional route fails legal, KYC, compliance, customer or financial-readiness gates.

When stopping, return the reason, the evidence gap and the cheapest next experiment.
