# BD outreach brief

## Subject

`[Specific workload] × [Ecosystem] — [measurable outcome]`

## Message

Hi [name],

We are building [one-sentence product] for [specific customer/workload]. We are exploring [ecosystem] because [native technical/distribution reason].

Current proof:

- [deployment / users / volume / pilot]
- [integration or workflow]
- [security/compliance constraint]

The concrete proposal is [pilot/integration/partner route], measured by [KPI] over [timeframe]. We are looking for [technical co-design / customer intro / integration / commercial support], not a generic grant.

Would [specific next step] be relevant for your [team/function]? If not, who owns this workload?

Best,
[name]

## Follow-up sequence

1. Day 0: send one-page brief.
2. Day 4: add one new proof point or artifact.
3. Day 10: ask for the correct owner and close the loop.

Stop after three high-quality follow-ups without a champion.
